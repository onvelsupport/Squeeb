Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

SQUEEB respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect your username, name, email address, wallet activity, marketplace activity, social task activity, bank payout details, device information, and payment-related information.

3. Payments

Payments may be processed by third-party providers such as Stripe. SQUEEB does not store full card details.

4. Bank Details

If you request withdrawals, we may collect bank account name, bank name, sort code, and account number for payout processing.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to manage accounts, process payments, support withdrawals, prevent fraud, provide marketplace services, and improve SQUEEB.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact SQUEEB support through the support page.
